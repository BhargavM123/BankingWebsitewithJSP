What is special about online banking?

Internet banking is the most convenient way to bank- anytime, any place, at your convenience.

I do not have a PC?

You can access our website from any device that has connectivity to the Internet.

Will there be any charges to signing up for Online Banking?

No, there are no charges for signing up.

Who can use Online Banking?

Customers having a working account under OTB can access online banking.

How safe is Online Banking?

OTB takes every precaution to keep your online experience safe. In a continued effort to safeguard online banking transactions, OTB requires full 128-bit encryption for Personal Internet Banking use. Encryption is a process that transforms sensitive information into a string of unrecognizable characters before they are sent over the Internet and helps keep your information private between the bank's computer system and your Internet browser.

Is Online Banking available 24*7?

The Online Banking service is available round-the-clock, seven days a week.

Is my password for Online Banking case sensitive?

Your password is case sensitive. As you go through the registration journey, you will be guided accordingly on the password criteria.

What do I need in order to use Online Banking services?

A device with Internet connection. Any operating software: Internet Explorer or Chrome (latest version recommended to have full compatibility with our system).
